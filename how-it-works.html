One platform, two experiences.

Skiip bridges the gap between hungry attendees and busy vendors, ensuring everyone spends more time enjoying the festival.

For Attendees

1. Scan & Discover

No app download required. Scan a QR code at any vendor or stage to see the full menu instantly.

2. Order & Pay

Select your items and pay securely in seconds. We support all major digital wallets.

3. Relax & React

Keep an eye on the live queue tracker. We'll ping you when it's time to head to the pickup window.

For Vendors

1. Order Intake

Orders stream directly to your tablet. No manual entry, no misheard requests, no cash handling.

2. Preparation

Update order status with a single tap. The system automatically manages the queue sequence.

3. Fulfillment

Mark as 'Ready' to notify the customer. Scan their digital pickup code for a secure handoff.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are there extra fees for attendees?

We charge a small platform fee per transaction, typically shared between the vendor and the attendee. This covers the cost of secure payments and the queue tracking infrastructure.

What if the internet goes down?

Skiip is built with offline-resilience. Our vendor hubs create a local network mesh that keeps orders flowing even if the main venue Wi-Fi or cellular network is congested.

How do refunds work?

Refunds can be initiated directly by the vendor from their dashboard if an item is out of stock. The funds are returned to the attendee's original payment method automatically.
